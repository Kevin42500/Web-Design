About Us

At Scobett Motors, we are dedicated to providing the finest luxury and performance vehicles. Our journey began in 2020 with the aim of bringing the best automotive experiences to enthusiasts and customers worldwide. Located along Ngong Road, we pride ourselves on offering vehicles that are not only in excellent condition but also priced to ensure our customers get the best value for their money.

Our Story

Founded by car enthusiasts, Scobett Motors has grown from a small dealership to a renowned name in the luxury car market. Our commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and innovation has driven our success over the years.

Since our inception, we have focused on creating a unique car-buying experience. Our team of knowledgeable professionals is passionate about cars and dedicated to helping you find the perfect vehicle. Whether you are looking for a sleek sports car, a luxurious sedan, or a rugged SUV, we have something to suit every taste and need.

Our Philosophy

At Scobett Motors, we believe in maintaining the highest standards of service and customer care. We understand that buying a car is a significant investment, and we strive to make the process as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Our vehicles undergo rigorous inspections to ensure they meet our exacting standards for quality and performance.

Customer satisfaction is at the heart of everything we do. We offer competitive, customer-friendly prices to ensure that you receive the best value for your money. Our commitment to transparency and integrity means you can trust us to provide honest advice and support throughout your car-buying journey.

Community Involvement

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future, Scobett Motors remains dedicated to innovation and excellence. We continuously update our inventory to include the latest models and cutting-edge technologies.
